Herosubtitle: From Field to Glass

----

Herotext: Every bottle of Tequila Los Valores begins in the agave fields of Jalisco and Nayarit, where we hand-select Tequilana Weber Blue Agave from both the lowlands and highlands. Each plant takes between five and seven years to reach full maturity before being harvested and brought to our distillery.
